Context

I had to work on game involved picking actions to go about your day and i did this with a partner. The way we had to make it was by adding links to files which would lead to other files and so on.

Content

My partner and I made our game based on the Oddesey and you start your journey in your howntown, where you can either go fight in a war or stay at home. The fight in war side had you try and get back home after winning the war, while the staying at home side made you fight men who are trying to stealy our wife away. I worked on the fighting in war side and my partner did all the files on the other side. We seperated our files into two folders, one being all the files that I was doing, and all the files my partner was doing. In these files we explained what was happening and actions you could choose that would be links to other files
